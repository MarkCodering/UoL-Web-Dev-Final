Taiwan's Internet History

The Dawn of TANet

The establishment of TANet (Taiwan Academic Network) in 1991 was the first step, connecting universities and creating the foundation for the nation's digital future.

The Age of PTT

Founded in 1995, the PTT bulletin board system became a central hub for news and community, shaping a unique and vibrant online culture in Taiwan.

The Mobile Era & Future

Driven by smartphone adoption and local apps like LINE, Taiwan's digital landscape shifted. Now, its semiconductor industry positions it to lead in AI and Web3.
